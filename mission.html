    space missions

    Apollo 11

    Agency: NASA
    First mission to land humans on the Moon.
    Astronauts Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin walked on the lunar surface. Objective: Manned lunar landing and safe return to Earth.
    Launch Date: July 16, 1969.

    Curiosity Rover

    Agency: NASA
    Robotic rover exploring Mars’ surface since 2012.
    Studies the planet’s climate, geology, and habitability. Objective: Search for signs of past life and water.
    Launch Date: November 26, 2011.

    Artemis I

    Agency: NASA
    Uncrewed test flight around the Moon as part of Artemis program.
    Prepares future crewed lunar missions. Objective: Test the Space Launch System and Orion spacecraft..
    Launch Date: November 16, 2022.

    Voyager 1

    Agency: NASA / ESA / CSA
    The most powerful space telescope ever built
    Observes galaxies, stars, and exoplanets in infrared light. Objective: Study the early universe and planet formation
    Launch Date: December 25, 2021.

    Mars Express

    Agency: NASA
    Deep space probe traveling beyond our Solar System.
    Carries the “Golden Record” with sounds and images of Earth. Objective: Study outer planets and interstellar space.
    Launch Date: July 16, 1969.
